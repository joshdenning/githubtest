Da Sunz

Da Green Sunz are an Ironjawz tribe that split from the main faction generations ago.
Living to quench their thirst for brutality Da Sunz move from realm to realm looking for the next scrap.

Da Sunz differ from other Ironjawz tribes in that they are not lead my a Megaboss but are in fact led by Weirdnob Shaman Jaggspudz.
Jaggspudz lends his control of the WAAAAGH energy to warchanters and Megabosses to corrupt more Orruks into joining his ranks.
