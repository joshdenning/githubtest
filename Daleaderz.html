Da Sunz are lead by Weirdnob Shaman Jaggspudz, Jaggspudz is a powerful spellcaster with a higher level of intelligence than an average Orruk.

He has lead Da Sunz to worship him as a god and 'gifts' his followers by infusing them with WAAGH! magic making them much stronger and increasing their bloodlust.

Jaggspudz' use of the empowering magic can lead to accidents that leave Orruks mutated both physically and mentally.

A Mawkrusha is a giant brutish wyvern that many Ironjawz clans attempt to tame for their bosses to ride into battle.

Da Sunz are no different having enslaved the beast Mightypeet. Jaggspudz second in command Megaboss GazSnak rides the beast into battle.

Bellowing orders and running head first into battle laying waste to any foolish enough to stand in their way GazSnak and Mightypeet make their way through battlefield to battlefield.
